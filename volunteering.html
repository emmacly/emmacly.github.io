My Experience in Leadership

Ban Khuyen Hoc (BKH)

Board of Directors | Calgary, AB| 2025 – Present

Overview

Ban Khuyen Hoc (BKH), also known as the Calgary Vietnamese Association for Encouragement of Learning (CVAEL), is a non-profit organization dedicated to breaking down barriers and increasing access to educational resources for students of Vietnamese descent. Through free tutoring programs and scholarships, the organization empowers students and fosters academic success within the Vietnamese community in Calgary.

Leadership Contributions

  • Oversee the day-to-day operations of the tutoring program
  • Support and coordinate volunteer tutors to ensure effective program delivery
  • Manage resources and supplies to maintain an organized, accessible learning environment
  • Ensure a supportive and structured space for students

Impact & Growth

Through my leadership roles at BKH, I have developed strong organizational, communication, and strategic planning skills. Balancing program coordination and marketing has strengthened my ability to manage multiple responsibilities while maintaining a clear focus on community impact. Most importantly, this role has reinforced my commitment to fostering inclusive, empowering educational spaces that support student success.

Vietnamese Students' Association (VSA)

Vice President | University of Calgary | 2023 – Present

Overview

The Vietnamese Students’ Association (VSA) is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering community among Vietnamese students and creating a safe space to explore cultural identity. The club hosts large-scale cultural and recreational events throughout the academic year.

Leadership Contributions

  • Oversee a team of 30+ executives, providing mentorship and strategic direction.
  • Designed and implemented a comprehensive annual event roadmap to ensure continuity and organizational efficiency.
  • Spearheaded large-scale cultural programming, including Hoi Cho Tet and the VSA Volleyball Tournament.
  • Led events drawing 300–1,400 attendees, including dignitaries such as Mayor Jyoti Gondek and Members of Parliament.
  • Supported smaller engagement initiatives including bake sales, movie nights, and craft nights.

Impact & Growth

In this role, I strengthened my executive leadership, delegation, and long-term planning skills. Developing standardized processes and overseeing large-scale events enhanced my ability to lead with structure and vision. Most importantly, I learned how intentional leadership can build meaningful community impact.

Calgary Board of Education

Summer Intern | Employee Health Resource Centre | Summer 2024 & 2025

Overview

During the summer of 2024, I worked within the Employee Health Resource Centre (EHRC) at the Calgary Board of Education, supporting occupational health and return-to-work processes across the district.

Leadership Contributions

  • Conducted over 100 interviews with CBE staff across 60+ positions
  • Created 45+ completed Job Demand Assessments (JDAs) outlining physical and cognitive job requirements
  • Collaborated with employees to document workplace demands for use in return-to-work and accommodation planning
  • Developed strong professional communication and documentation skills through iterative feedback and revision

Impact & Growth

This experience deepened my understanding of workplace accessibility, accommodation processes, and the physical demands placed on educators and staff. It strengthened my confidence in professional settings and reaffirmed my goal of becoming an educator within the CBE.
