Hi, I'm Emma!

I am a concurrent Kinesiology and Education major who is passionate about creating safe, positive, and supportive learning environments. I believe teachers play a key role in helping students thrive—not only academically, but as growing individuals. My goal is to nurture students’ potential by fostering classrooms where they feel valued, empowered, and encouraged to explore. I am dedicated, optimistic, and love building meaningful connections with others. With experience across multiple fields, I bring strong leadership, teamwork, and adaptability to every space I step into, both as an educator and as a learner.

01

Building Community

I believe that building a safe, positive, and supportive community is one of the most important aspects of any classroom. When students feel welcomed and valued, they are more willing to engage with academic content, take risks, and connect with their peers. By fostering an environment where every student feels they belong, we create the foundation for meaningful learning and unlock each learner’s full potential.

02

Growth Through Collaboration

While being independent is an essential skill in the workforce, I believe that we grow the most when we are collaborating with others. Through our interactions with our peers and colleagues, we can better understand not only those around us, but also ourselves. This allows for continuous growth, as we allow others to challenge our beliefs and provide us with outside perspectives that we may not have initially considered.

03

Fostering Healthy Living

As a proud Kinesiology Major at the University of Calgary, I firmly believe in the importance of physical activity and the powerful role it can play in supporting students—not only physically, but academically as well. I believe movement has a place in all classrooms, and that integrating activity can help students stay focused while also creating inclusive spaces where everyone feels welcome, regardless of socioeconomic background or lived experience.
